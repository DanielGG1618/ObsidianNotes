## Definition
- *Partial Order* is a [[Binary Relation]] which is:
	- Composable
	- [[Associativity|Associative]]
	- [[Reflexivity|Reflexive]] 
	and 
	- $\forall a, b: aRb => \neg bRa$ (without this it is [[Preorder]])

- So, every [[Preorder]] is a [[Partial Order]]
