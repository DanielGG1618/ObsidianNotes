## Test
$$\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{|a_nx^n|}=\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{|a_n|}\cdot|x|<1$$
$$\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{|a_n|}=\rho\implies|x|<\frac1\rho$$

- Power series converge for any $x$ such that $|x|<\frac 1\rho$ and diverges for $|x|>\frac1\rho$
