## Definition
- The sum $$c_n=\sum_{k=0}^na_kb_{n-k}$$
is called the *convolution*
## Example
$$a_n=(-1)^n,\; b_n=\frac{1}{n!}$$
$$c_0=1, c_1=1-1=0,c_2=\frac12-1+1=\frac12, c_3=\frac16-\frac12+\frac11-\frac11=\frac13$$
$$c_n=\sum_{k=0}^n\frac{(-1)^k}{(n-k)!}$$
