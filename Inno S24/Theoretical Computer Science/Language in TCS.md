## Definitions
- The set of all [[String in TCS|strings]] over [[Alphabet in TCS|alphabet]] $\Sigma$ is denoted by $\Sigma^*$
	- Example:
$$\Sigma=\{0,1\}\implies\Sigma^*=\{\varepsilon,0,1,00,01,10,11,000,001,...\}$$
- A *language* $L$ is a set of [[String in TCS|strings]] over an [[Alphabet in TCS|alphabet]] $\Sigma$. ($L\subseteq \Sigma^*$)
