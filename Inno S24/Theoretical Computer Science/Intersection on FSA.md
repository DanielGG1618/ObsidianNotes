## Formally 
- Given 
	- $A^1=<Q^1, I, \delta^1, q_0^1, F^1>$
	- $A^2=<Q^2, I, \delta^2, q_0^2, F^2>$
- We get
	-  $<A^1, A^2>=<Q^1\times Q^2, I, \delta, (q_0^1, q_0^2), F^1\times F^2>$
		- $\delta((q^1, q^2), i) = (\delta(q_1,i),\delta^2(q_2, i))$
	- $L(A^1)\cap L(A^2) = L(<A^1, A^2>)$
