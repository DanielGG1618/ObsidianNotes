## Definition
- A [[Sequence]] $x_0, x_1, x_2, ...$ is a (partial) solution of a [[Linear Recurrence Sequence]] $a_{n+k} = t_1a_{n+k-1} +...+t_ka_n$, if the recursion holds for $a_n=x_n$
## Example
$$a_{n+2}=a_{n+1}+a_n$$$$0,1,1,2,3,5,8,13,...$$$$7, 3, 10, 13, 23, 36,59,...$$
