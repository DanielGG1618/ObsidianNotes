## Definition
$\left(\begin{matrix}n\\ k\end{matrix}\right) = \large\frac{n!}{n!(n-k)!}$
## Properties
1. $\Large\binom{n}{n} = \frac{n!}{n!(n-n)!} = 1$
2. $\Large\binom{n}{0} = \frac{n!}{0!(n-0)!} = 1$
$\forall k \leq n$
3. $\Large\binom{n}{k}=\binom{n}{n-k}$
4. $\Large\binom{n}{k} = \binom{n-1}{k-1}+\binom{n-1}{k}$
