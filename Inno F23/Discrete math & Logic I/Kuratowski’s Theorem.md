## Lemma
- A [[Graph]] is a [[Planar Graph]] $\iff$ its [[Subdivision]]s are [[Planar Graph]]s
## Theorem
- A [[Graph]] is a [[Planar Graph]] $\iff$ it does not contain a [[Subgraph]] that is a [[Subdivision]] of $K_5$ or $K_{3,3}$
