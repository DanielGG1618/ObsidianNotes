## Definition
- Another name is [[Modus Ponens|Law of Detachment]]
$$\begin{array}{l} P\\ P\implies Q\\ \hline Q\end{array}$$
## Proof
| $P$ | $Q$ | $P \implies Q$ |
| --- | --- | -------------- |

| 0   | 1   | 1              |
| 1   | 0   | 0              |
