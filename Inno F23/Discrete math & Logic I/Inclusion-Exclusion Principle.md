## Definition
$\displaystyle|A_1\cup A_2\cup...\cup A_n| = \sum^n_{i=1}|A_i|-\sum^n_{i,j=(i<j)}|A_i\cap A_j| +$
$\displaystyle +\sum^n_{(i,j,k=1(i<j<k))}|A_i\cap A_j\cap A_k| - ... + (-1)^{n+1}|A_1\cap A_2\cap...\cap A_n|$

## Example
$|A_1 ∪ A_2 ∪ A_3| = |A_1| + |A_2| - |A_1\cap A_2|$
