## Definition
Let $a<b$ be real numbers or $\pm\infty$; let us define an *antiderivative* of a [[Function]] $f$.
$f:]a,b[\to\mathbb R$ as any [[Function]] $F:]a,b[\to\mathbb R$ such [[Derivative|that]] $F'(x)=f(x)$ at any point $x\in]a,b[$
